Privacy Policy

HairCut History

Last Updated: December 10, 2025

Introduction

HairCut History ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, password (stored securely)
  • Profile Information: Haircut profile names, measurements, preferences, and notes
  • Photos: Reference photos you upload for haircut profiles
  • Barber Information: Shop/salon name, professional notes (if using Barber mode)

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device type, model, and operating system
  • Unique device identifiers
  • App usage and session data
  • Error logs and crash reports

How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Store and display your haircut profiles
  • Enable QR code sharing with barbers
  • Sync data across your devices
  • Improve the App and develop new features
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on:

  • PlayFab servers (Microsoft Azure): Account and profile data
  • Azure Blob Storage: Photos (if cloud sync enabled)
  • Your device: Local cache for offline access

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS/SSL), secure password hashing (PBKDF2-SHA256), and token-based authentication.

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your data.

We may share information only:

  • With barbers when you generate and share a QR code (your choice)
  • With service providers (Microsoft Azure, PlayFab) bound by confidentiality
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

Your Rights

  • Access: View all data associated with your account
  • Update: Modify your profile and preferences
  • Delete: Delete profiles or your entire account
  • Export: Request a copy of your data

Exercise your rights in the App settings or contact privacy@haircuthistory.app
