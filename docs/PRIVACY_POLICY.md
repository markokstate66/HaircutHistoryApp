# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 10, 2025**

## Introduction

HairCut History ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

1. **Account Information**
   - Email address
   - Display name
   - Password (stored securely using industry-standard hashing)

2. **Profile Information**
   - Haircut profile names and descriptions
   - Haircut measurements and preferences
   - General notes about your haircuts

3. **Photos**
   - Reference photos you upload for haircut profiles

4. **Barber Information** (if using Barber mode)
   - Shop/salon name
   - Professional notes about clients

### Information Collected Automatically

1. **Device Information**
   - Device type and model
   - Operating system version
   - Unique device identifiers

2. **Usage Data**
   - App feature usage
   - Session duration
   - Interaction patterns

3. **Log Data**
   - Error logs and crash reports
   - Performance data

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide Services**
   - Create and manage your account
   - Store and display your haircut profiles
   - Enable QR code sharing with barbers
   - Sync data across your devices

2. **Improve the App**
   - Analyze usage patterns
   - Fix bugs and improve performance
   - Develop new features

3. **Communicate with You**
   - Send service-related notifications
   - Respond to support requests
   - Provide updates about the App

4. **Ensure Security**
   - Detect and prevent fraud
   - Protect against unauthorized access
   - Comply with legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

### Where We Store Your Data

- **Account Data**: Stored securely on PlayFab servers (Microsoft Azure)
- **Profile Data**: Stored securely on PlayFab servers
- **Photos**: Stored on Azure Blob Storage (if cloud sync is enabled) or locally on your device
- **Local Data**: Some data is cached locally on your device for offline access

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Secure password hashing (PBKDF2-SHA256)
- Secure token-based authentication
- Regular security audits

## Data Sharing

### We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

### When We Share Data

We may share your information only in these circumstances:

1. **With Barbers (Your Choice)**
   - When you generate a QR code to share your haircut profile
   - Sharing is temporary (24-hour expiration)
   - You control what information is shared

2. **Service Providers**
   - Cloud hosting providers (Microsoft Azure, PlayFab)
   - These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements

3. **Legal Requirements**
   - When required by law
   - To protect our rights or safety
   - To prevent fraud or abuse

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data associated with your account
- **Update**: Modify your profile and preferences
- **Delete**: Delete individual profiles or your entire account
- **Export**: Request a copy of your data

### How to Exercise Your Rights

- **In-App**: Use the Settings page to manage your data
- **Account Deletion**: Go to Settings > Delete Account
- **Contact Us**: Email privacy@haircuthistory.app

### Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data is retained while your account is active
- **Deleted Data**: Permanently deleted within 30 days of deletion request
- **Backups**: Removed from backups within 90 days

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.
