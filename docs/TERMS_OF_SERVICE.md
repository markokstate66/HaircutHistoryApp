## QR Code Sharing

### How Sharing Works

When you generate a QR code:
- A temporary share session is created (valid for 24 hours)
- The QR code contains a unique session identifier
- Barbers who scan the code can view your selected profile
- You can revoke access at any time

### Barber Access

When a barber scans your QR code, they can:
- View your haircut profile information
- Add professional notes to your profile
- The information shared is limited to the selected profile

### Your Responsibility

You are responsible for:
- Choosing what information to share
- Only sharing QR codes with trusted barbers
